Who We Are

Indore's Premier JEE & NEET Coaching Institute

Biotrons Career Institute is a leading coaching center in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, dedicated to preparing students for NEET and JEE examinations. Located at Vibrant Tower, Manorama Ganj, we have built a reputation for delivering consistent top ranks and transforming aspirants into achievers.

Founded with the vision of making quality coaching accessible to students in Central India, Biotrons combines Kota-level teaching methodology with the warmth and personal attention that Indore families value. Our tagline — JEE Institute in Indore | NEET Institute in Indore — reflects our singular focus on these two life-changing examinations.

Under the banner of Biotrons Education Pvt Ltd., we serve hundreds of students across 11th, 12th, and dropper batches, offering flexible timings from evening school-going batches to intensive 8-to-8 Gurukul programs.

🔭 Our Vision

To be the most trusted and result-oriented JEE & NEET coaching institute in Central India, empowering every student to achieve their full potential and secure admission to premier medical and engineering institutions including AIIMS, IITs, and top NITs.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality coaching with expert faculty, innovative teaching methods, and a nurturing environment that builds not just exam-ready students, but confident, capable individuals ready to excel in life.
